About the Author

Claire Harmon writes for women in the middle of perimenopause who are exhausted, confused, and ready for real answers. Not the "this is totally normal, just exercise more" answers. Real ones. Her workbooks address what's actually happening: the anxiety that arrives out of nowhere, the brain fog that makes you feel like you're losing your mind, the sleep that disappeared, and the emotions that no longer follow any predictable pattern.

Her approach combines the latest research on hormonal health with practical, day-by-day tools that help women understand their own patterns, advocate for themselves with their doctors, and find strategies that work for their specific experience â€” because perimenopause looks different for every woman.
